Inventory

In the Inventory tab you can manage your inventory using these features:

  • List all Apparatus: view the list of your current apparatus and newly added apparatus.
  • Add new Apparatus: to add a apparatus on your inventory.
  • Update Apparatus Record: to edit or update the status of your item in the inventory.
  • Delete Apparatus Record: remove undesired apparatus on your inventory.

    • Borrow

    In the Borrow tab you are able to do the following:

  • Record Borrowed Apparatus: Record the students who borrowed apparatus and store in local storage.
  • Update Quantity in Inventory: When a student borrows an apparatus, the data is updated where the quantity is subtracted depending on how many apparatus was borrowed.

    • Broken/Lost

    In the Broken/Lost tab you are able to do the following:

  • Record Broken/Lost Apparatus: Record the apparatus that have been damaged or lost by a student.
  • Update Quantity in Inventory: When a student breaks or loses an apparatus, the data is updated where the quantity is subtracted depending on how many apparatus has been broken or lost.
